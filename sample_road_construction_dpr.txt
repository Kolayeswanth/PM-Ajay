DETAILED PROJECT REPORT (DPR)
PROJECT: CONSTRUCTION OF CONCRETE ROAD IN ADARSH GRAM

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
   - Project Name: Construction of CC Road from Main Highway to Village School
   - Scheme Component: Adarsh Gram (PM-AJAY)
   - District: Pune
   - Gram Panchayat: Shirur GP

2. TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
   - Length: 1.5 KM
   - Width: 3.75 Meters
   - Type: Cement Concrete (M30 Grade)
   - Shoulder: 1.0 Meter hard shoulder on both sides

3. ESTIMATED COST
   - Material Cost: ₹ 15,00,000
   - Labour Cost: ₹ 8,00,000
   - Machinery & Transport: ₹ 2,50,000
   - Total Estimated Cost: ₹ 25.50 Lakhs

4. JUSTIFICATION
   The existing road is a mud track which becomes inaccessible during monsoons. 
   This road connects the village to the primary school and healthcare center. 
   Construction is essential for all-weather connectivity.

5. IMPLEMENTATION TIMELINE
   - Survey & Design: 1 Month
   - Tendering: 1 Month
   - Execution: 4 Months
   - Total Duration: 6 Months

Submitted by: District Administration
Date: November 30, 2025
